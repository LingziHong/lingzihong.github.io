Hi, Welcome to my HCC lab site.

Our goal is to develop innovative methodologies for understanding and enhancing the interaction between humans and sociotechnical systems. The aim is to create technologies that are innovative and deeply attuned to human needs and behaviors.

Interdisciplinary approaches, including advanced computing techniques, quantitative analysis, qualitative user studies will be employed for a holistic examination of human perception and cognition, to decipher the complex interactions between humans and sociotechnical systems. The insights are applied for decision-making and creating intuitive and empowering digital applications. By focusing on user-centered computing, we ensure that technology serves to augment human potential, while being transparent and under control.

Here are the three main research focus:

  • Insights from Human-System Interaction for Decision Makings
  • Insights from Human-System Interaction for AI Development
  • Human-centered Evaluation of Emerging Technologies
